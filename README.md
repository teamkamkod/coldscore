# ColdScore — Quantified Cold Email Scoring

[![Live App](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-score.quick--polish.com-blue)](https://score.quick-polish.com)

**ColdScore** analyzes your cold emails and gives you a quantified score out of 100. Instead of vague advice like "make it more personal," you get specific, dimension-level scores and actionable fixes.

## Why ColdScore?

ChatGPT can write emails. But can it tell you why your email will get ignored?

ColdScore fills the gap between "write something" and "send something effective":

- **Quantified feedback** — 0–100 score so you can measure progress
- **5 dimensions** — Personalization, Opening Hook, Value Proposition, CTA, Conciseness
- **Specific reasons** — not "needs work" but "Personalization: 12/25 — no recipient name or company reference"
- **Rewritten version** — see what a high-scoring version looks like
- **No login** — paste your email, get your score in seconds

## Example

```
Your email → ColdScore → Score: 43/100

🎯 Personalization: 10/25 — No company or recipient research shown
🪝 Hook: 15/25 — Generic opener, focuses on you not them
💡 Value Prop: 12/25 — Benefit is vague, no specific metric
📞 CTA: 6/25 — Unclear what the next step is
📝 +5 conciseness bonus (under 150 words)

⚠️ Top Issue: No specific personalization or proof of value
✨ Quick fix: Mention a specific company challenge or achievement
✍️ Rewritten version included
```
